FAQ

Is Quicklify free?

Yes — 100% free. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no "premium tier."

Is it safe?

The APK is built straight from this source repo and signed. No telemetry, no analytics SDKs, no account servers.

Why isn't it on Google Play?

Play Store policies restrict video-download apps. Sideloading the APK keeps the app free and unrestricted.

Does it work on iPhone?

Not yet — Quicklify is Android-only today. iOS support is on the roadmap.

Where do my downloads go?

Straight into your phone's Downloads / Movies folder, fully visible in your Gallery.
